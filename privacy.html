Privacy

How dhiphos.com handles the small amount of information we receive from visitors and inquiries.

Last updated: 29 May 2026

summary

This site uses Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how visitors find and move through our pages. We do not run advertising pixels, session-replay tools, or other third-party trackers, and we do not sell or share your data for marketing. Beyond analytics, the only personal data we receive is the email you choose to send us.

who we are

This website is operated by Dhiphos Private Limited, a company registered in Bengaluru, India.

what we collect

contact-form

The home-page contact form does not transmit data to our servers. It opens your mail client with a draft addressed to info@dhiphos.com; only sending that draft transmits anything.

direct-email

When you email us, we receive your email address and whatever you choose to include in the message body. We retain inquiry email for up to 24 months, after which it is deleted unless an active engagement exists or law requires longer retention.

server-logs

Our static-site host produces standard web-server logs (timestamp, IP address, user-agent, requested URL). These are kept briefly for security and abuse prevention. They are not joined to any other data set and are not used for advertising or profiling.

analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 (property G-GHVMTXCPLV) to measure aggregate traffic — pages viewed, referrers, approximate region, device and browser type, and similar usage signals. Google Analytics sets cookies in your browser and processes a truncated version of your IP address; it does not receive your name, email, or other directly identifying information from us. Google acts as a data processor on our behalf, and may also process the data as an independent controller under its own terms; see Google's privacy policy and business privacy notices for details. You can opt out by installing Google's Analytics opt-out browser add-on, by blocking analytics cookies in your browser, or by using a tracker-blocking extension.

cookies

We do not set first-party cookies of our own. The only cookies set on this site come from Google Analytics (typically _ga and _ga_*), used to distinguish unique visitors and sessions. We do not use advertising pixels or session-replay tools.

what we use it for

We use what you send us only to respond to your inquiry and to deliver the products or services you've asked about. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing.

hosting & third parties

hosting

The site is hosted on GitHub Pages (operated by GitHub, Inc.) and served from a global CDN, which may briefly route requests through nodes outside India. The host receives the standard server-log fields described above; GitHub's privacy practices apply to those logs.

analytics-provider

Google Analytics is provided by Google LLC (and, for visitors in the EEA / UK / Switzerland, Google Ireland Limited). Analytics data may be processed on servers outside India, including in the United States and the European Union, under Google's standard contractual terms.

email-provider

Email sent to info@dhiphos.com is processed by Zoho Mail, our mailbox provider, who acts as a data processor on our behalf.

your rights

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (India) and comparable laws (including the EU GDPR), you have the right to access the personal data we hold about you, ask us to correct or delete it, and withdraw consent for any processing that depends on it. To exercise any of these rights, email info@dhiphos.com. We aim to respond within 30 days.

children

This website and our services are not directed at people under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

changes

If we update this notice, we will post the new version on this page and refresh the “Last updated” date at the top.
