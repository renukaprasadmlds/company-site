Privacy

How dhiphos.com handles the small amount of information we receive from visitors and inquiries.

Last updated: 27 May 2026

summary

This site doesn't track you. We don't set cookies, run analytics, or load tracking pixels. The only personal data we receive is what you send us by email — either through the contact form on the home page, which simply opens your mail client with a draft, or by emailing info@dhiphos.com directly. That email is used to respond to your inquiry, and nothing else.

who we are

This website is operated by Dhiphos Private Limited, a company registered in Bengaluru, India. For any privacy-related question, contact us at info@dhiphos.com.

what we collect

contact-form

The contact form on the home page does not transmit any data to our servers. It validates the email address you typed and then opens your default email client with a short pre-filled draft addressed to info@dhiphos.com. Whether you choose to send that draft is entirely up to you.

direct-email

When you email us, we receive your email address and whatever you choose to include in the message body. We retain this only for as long as needed to respond and to keep a reasonable record of the correspondence.

server-logs

Our static-site host produces standard web-server logs (timestamp, IP address, user-agent, requested URL). These are kept briefly for security and abuse prevention. They are not joined to any other data set and are not used for advertising or profiling.

no-cookies

We do not set any cookies. We do not use Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, Hotjar, or any similar tracking service.

what we use it for

We use what you send us only to respond to your inquiry and to deliver the products or services you've asked about. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing.

hosting & third parties

hosting

The site is hosted on GitHub Pages (operated by GitHub, Inc.). The host receives the standard server-log fields described above. GitHub's privacy practices apply to those logs.

linkedin

Our footer links to our LinkedIn company page. The link opens in a new tab. We don't embed LinkedIn widgets or trackers on this site, so simply viewing dhiphos.com does not share data with LinkedIn.

email-provider

Email sent to info@dhiphos.com is processed by Zoho Mail, our mailbox provider, who acts as a data processor on our behalf.

your rights

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (India) and comparable laws (including the EU GDPR), you have the right to access the personal data we hold about you, ask us to correct or delete it, and withdraw consent for any processing that depends on it. To exercise any of these rights, email info@dhiphos.com. We aim to respond within 30 days.

children

This website and our services are not directed at people under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

changes

If we update this notice, we will post the new version on this page and refresh the “Last updated” date at the top. Material changes will be highlighted briefly on the home page.
